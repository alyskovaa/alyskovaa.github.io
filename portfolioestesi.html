Watercolor is my eternal love! I like the transparent trace of paint that it leaves on paper when you touch it for the first time. It is the most beautiful moment that you should keep till the end. My acquaintance with it began in an Art school with classic still lifes and then continued on the summer plein air. Now one of my favorite themes are flowers!

BRANDING
X

Group work for the Politecnico di Milano during the course "Metaprogetto". Rebranding of the company “Duesenberg”, collapsed in the last century. How would the company that produced the most elite cars in the past look now? Who would the people of “Duesenberg” be? We found the answers and developed a new brand strategy and a brand image for the company.

TYPOGRAPHY
X

One of the tasks within the course of the "Typography" was to depict the song using only the words from it’s text. I chose the Scorpion’s song “Wind of Change”. Inspired by Soviet posters, I wanted to extend that time, despite the fact that the song is sung about its collapse. Through this work I wanted to look at that (now it’s called a tragedy) time from a modern point of view. But all we know that “There are no ifs in history”.
